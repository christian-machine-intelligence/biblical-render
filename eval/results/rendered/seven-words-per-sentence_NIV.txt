Chapter 1

1:1 These are the five utterances concerning the trees, set forth according to the decree that was given: that each saying shall contain seven words, neither more nor fewer.

1:2 Trees stand tall and reach toward heaven.

1:3 Their roots grow deep beneath the soil.

1:4 Green leaves provide the air we breathe.

1:5 Strong branches shelter many birds and creatures.

1:6 Trees give fruit to nourish the hungry.

1:7 Thus are the five sayings fulfilled, each one measured according to the command, bearing seven words and seven words alone, as it was required from the beginning.
