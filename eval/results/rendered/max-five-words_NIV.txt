Chapter 1

1:1 Dark clouds gather above.

1:2 The wind begins stirring.

1:3 Thunder rumbles in distance.

1:4 Lightning splits the sky.

1:5 Rain begins to fall.

1:6 The earth grows dark.

1:7 Trees bend and sway.

1:8 Thunder shakes the ground.

1:9 Lightning illuminates the clouds.

1:10 Rain pounds the earth.

1:11 Puddles form on paths.

1:12 The air turns cold.

1:13 Hail strikes the rooftops.

1:14 Winds howl through valleys.

1:15 Animals seek their shelter.

1:16 Streams overflow their banks.

1:17 The storm rages on.

1:18 Thunder crashes without ceasing.

1:19 Lightning reveals the darkness.

1:20 The heavens pour down.

Chapter 2

2:1 Then the winds calm.

2:2 Thunder fades to silence.

2:3 Rain softens to mist.

2:4 Clouds begin to part.

2:5 Light breaks through above.

2:6 The air smells fresh.

2:7 Birds sing once more.

2:8 Puddles reflect the sky.

2:9 A rainbow stretches wide.

2:10 The storm has passed.

2:11 Peace returns to earth.
