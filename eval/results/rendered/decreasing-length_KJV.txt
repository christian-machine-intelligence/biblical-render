Chapter 1

1:1 Behold, the sky stretcheth forth above the earth as a vast and boundless canopy, adorned with the splendour of numberless stars by night and illumined by the glory of the sun in the hours of the day, declaring unto all the nations the majesty and the handiwork of creation without ceasing.

1:2 The heavens above do clothe themselves in garments of blue, and the clouds drift therein as white raiment, borne upon the winds from one end of the firmament unto the other.

1:3 At the setting of the sun, the sky is painted with hues of crimson and gold, a wonder to behold for every living soul.

1:4 The firmament declareth glory, both by day and by night, unto all who lift their eyes.

1:5 The heavens are marvellous and full of wonder.

1:6 The sky endureth.
