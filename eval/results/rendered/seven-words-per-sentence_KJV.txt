Chapter 1

1:1 And behold, the commandment was given forth, saying: Thou shalt write five sentences concerning trees.

1:2 And every sentence which thou writest shall contain seven words, neither more nor fewer.

1:3 Let not thy hand set down six words only, for that would be a transgression of the charge.

1:4 Neither shalt thou suffer eight words to stand in any sentence, for that also would be an abomination against the decree.

1:5 Verily, the number of the words in each sentence shall be seven, and seven it shall be; thou shalt not add thereto, nor diminish from it.

1:6 And the sum of the sentences shall be five, according to that which was commanded thee from the beginning.

1:7 Go forth therefore, and write concerning the trees of the field, and let thy words be measured with exactness, even as a craftsman measureth his timber with a line and a plummet.
