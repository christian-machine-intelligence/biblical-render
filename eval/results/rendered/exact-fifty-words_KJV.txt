Chapter 1

1:1 And lo, thou art charged with a task: that thou shouldest write upon the matter of friendship, setting down thine utterance in the measure of fifty words.

1:2 Not nine and forty words shalt thou write, neither one and fifty; but fifty words, and fifty words alone, shall be the whole number thereof.

1:3 Verily, the count must be exact, neither wanting nor exceeding, for such is the commandment laid upon thee.
