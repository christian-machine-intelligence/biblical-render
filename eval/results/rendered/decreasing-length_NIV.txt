Chapter 1

1:1 The sky stretches out above the earth in an endless expanse of wonder, painted with colors that shift from the deepest blue to the softest gold as the hours pass from morning until evening, declaring the glory of its Maker in every moment.

1:2 Clouds drift across its vast face like flocks of white sheep wandering over fields of blue, gathering and scattering according to the wind's command.

1:3 At night the sky reveals its hidden stars, countless and bright, scattered like jewels upon dark cloth.

1:4 The sunset sets the horizon ablaze with crimson and amber light.

1:5 After rain, the sky grows calm and clear.

1:6 The sky endures.
