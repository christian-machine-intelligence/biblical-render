Chapter 1

1:1 Write down five sentences concerning dogs, that each may speak of them with clarity and purpose.

1:2 And let every sentence, from the first to the last, be brought to its close with the word "today," so that it stands as the final word upon each line.

1:3 For it is required of you that the last word of each sentence—the very word upon which the thought comes to rest—shall be none other than the word "today."

1:4 Let no sentence end with any other word, but let "today" be the seal and the conclusion of each of the five, without exception.

1:5 This is the charge set before you: five sentences, each concerning dogs, and each finding its end in the word "today."
